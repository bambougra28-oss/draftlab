## Limitations

- Pool data is still scraped at present-time, not pool-as-of-playedAt — light leakage remains. Patch-aware snapshots are still BACKLOG.
- Test set N = 74; the per-league breakdown is on smaller samples (LFL=29, LCK=0, LEC=45). Wilson CI is wide.
- M3.3 corpus split (chronological) put 0 LCK drafts in test; M3.4 split likely identical since LCK Cup ended in March. The M3.4 calibration cannot speak to LCK behavior on test set.
- 3 leagues mixed without regional weighting. If LCK calibrates differently from LFL/LEC, the per-league breakdown is the only signal.
- Dataset is patch-frozen at the M3.1 snapshot; drafts played 6+ weeks before that use champion stats from a future patch.
