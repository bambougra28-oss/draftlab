## Limitations

- LCK Rounds 1-2 and LPL Split 2 are still in progress; pool data captured at scrape time reflects whatever has been played up to now, not the pool as-of each draft. Patch-aware snapshots remain BACKLOG.
- N test = 96; per-league N is smaller (LFL=10, LEC=16, LCK=30, LPL=40). Wilson CI is wide on the smaller buckets.
- Dataset is patch-frozen at the M3.1 snapshot; older drafts use future-patch champion stats.
- 4 leagues mixed without regional weighting. Per-league breakdown is the only way to see per-region behavior.
- LPL is traditionally early-game-aggressive; if its draft phase weighs blue/red sides differently from European splits, the side-only baseline may be calibrated differently per league.
